After signing up and becoming a member, the cycle pin purchased is Cycle 1, the next cycle pin you will purchase after your signup(cycle 1) is Cycle 2 and you will continue to move upward in the leadership level as you keep purchasing and activating your next cycle after building 6 team in the previous.

How many cycle pin can I purchase?

You can purchase as many as you want numbers of cycle activation pin after completing your 6 team each in the previous cycle. The more cycle pin you keep purchasing, the more you keep making money, with opportunity to qualify for other amazing prizes and rewards.
